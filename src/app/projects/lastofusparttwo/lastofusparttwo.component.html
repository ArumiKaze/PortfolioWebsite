Last of Us Part II

The Last of Us Part II is an AAA action-adventure game released in June 2020 for the Playstation 4. I was a Quality Assurance tester and a Japanese Localization specialist.

I created, maintained, and executed test cases through TestRail to investigate defects in the game, report/log issues with detailed reproduction steps including screenshots/videos using Jira, and thoroughly tested fixes that were made to ensure a quality product. Eventually, I took full QA ownership and responsibility for multiple levels and systems over multiple project cycles. In addition to that, I also mentored new personnel in quality assurance testing protocols and best practices to meet the studio's high standards.


On top of my quality assurance testing duty, I also worked on the Japanese localization of the game. I ensured quality and accurate translations from English to Japanese in text and voice lines, using a native's level of understanding of both languages along with an intimate knowledge of the game's lore to verify no nuance was lost.
