Shinguru

Shinguru is an upcoming 2D action-platformer game in which you embody an over-caffeinated mercenary slaying colossal demons while embarking on a journey through a world that mixes both sci-fi and fantasy. Through narratively driven levels, fluid combat, and parkour-esque movement, meet characters from all walks of life and learn that no one is ever truly alone when facing challenges life puts in your way.

I lead the programming department and implement a wide range of assets that requires C# in Unity. I primarily work on implementing player mechanics such as melee combat, locomotion, wall jumping, ledge climbing, etc. Development is still ongoing.
